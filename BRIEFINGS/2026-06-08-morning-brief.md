# Morning Brief — 2026-06-08

## THE ONE THING

Apple's WWDC 2026 keynote opened this morning in Cupertino — Tim Cook's final as CEO before John Ternus takes over — and delivered the AI pivot Apple has been delaying for two years. Siri has been rebuilt from scratch on a custom 1.2-trillion-parameter Google Gemini model (licensed at approximately $1 billion per year), turning it from a busted voice shortcut into a genuine conversational AI with personal context access (emails, messages, photos, files), on-screen awareness, multi-step commands, and a standalone chatbot-style app. The more structurally significant announcement for anyone tracking the AI landscape: Apple Intelligence Extensions. Users can now choose Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini as the AI powering their experience — system-wide, in every app — and developers can build Extensions that pick among these models too. Apple has just created the most consequential AI distribution event since ChatGPT launched: Claude is now a first-class option on over 2 billion active Apple devices.

---

## WHAT HAPPENED

- **Apple WWDC 2026 keynote (8 June)**: The keynote confirmed Siri rebuilt on Gemini with a new standalone Siri app, Dynamic Island integration on iPhone 16+, personal context awareness across apps, and support for multi-step cross-app commands. iOS 27, macOS 27, iPadOS 27, watchOS 27, tvOS 27, and visionOS 27 all announced. Apple Intelligence Extensions are the developer-facing implication: any app developer can now wire their product directly into Siri and designate which AI model (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) powers it. Tim Cook confirmed the deal on stage. John Ternus succeeds Cook as CEO — the first Apple CEO handover in 15 years.

- **Apple WWDC — implications for retail and commerce clients**: Siri historically failed as a commerce channel because it was too limited. A Gemini-backed Siri with cross-app action capabilities is a credible product discovery and shopping interface for the first time. Goldman Sachs sees the AI upgrade as a catalyst for a new iPhone replacement cycle — which means new device install base, new AI-commerce surface. For clients thinking about where AI-mediated product discovery shows up: the answer just expanded significantly. Siri joins ChatGPT Shopping and AI-powered search as a channel that brands will need to address explicitly, not just monitor.

- **Claude gains Apple-scale distribution**: Anthropic has been building scale through developers and enterprises. The Apple Extensions model is a qualitatively different kind of distribution: consumer-facing, default-installed on every new iPhone, chosen by the user. For clients who have been watching Claude's traction with scepticism, this is the signal that Claude is becoming infrastructure at device level — not just an enterprise tool.
